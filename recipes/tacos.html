Steak Tacos

Steak tacos only dropped once on the ground.

On the hunt for a satisfying dinner that everyone at your table will love? Try one of these crave-worthy recipes for steak tacos. Beefy and filling, we've got something for everyone — from marinated and grilled skirt steak to taqueria-style carne asada. No matter what you're in the mood for, you'll find something delicious in this collection of our favorite steak taco recipes.

Beat beef meat.

Ingredients

    1 cup chopped onion
    ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
    3 limes, juiced
    4 tablespoons soy sauce
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    4 teaspoons dark brown sugar
    2 teaspoons dried oregano
    1 ½ teaspoons red pepper flakes
    1 clove garlic, minced
    1 pound skirt steak, fat removed and flattened to 1/8-inch thickness
    4 corn tortillas

Steps

    1. Combine onion, cilantro, lime juice, soy sauce, olive oil, brown sugar, oregano, red pepper flakes, and garlic in a large bowl.
    2. Place steak in a baking pan and pour marinade on top. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 to 4 hours.
    3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
    4. Grill steak 2 to 4 minutes per side, to desired doneness. Allow meat to rest 5 to 10 minutes before slicing against the grain into 1/4-inch slices.
    5. Add meat to tortillas and top with your favorite toppings.
